About Us

Welcome to TECHIVE! We are a group of third-year Computer Engineering students from the University of Cebu, passionate about technology and innovation.

Our journey began as we explored the world of tech, and we decided to channel our knowledge and skills into creating a platform that offers the latest gadgets and tech-related products.

At TECHIVE, we strive to provide our customers with reliable products, exceptional service, and a unique shopping experience. Our goal is to bridge the gap between technology and the everyday user.

We hope you enjoy browsing our offerings as much as we enjoy bringing them to you. If you have any questions or feedback, please feel free to reach out to us!
